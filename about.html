about

I'm Diana Zhang and welcome to my awesome food blog. Every so often, I try creating new recipies and post it here to share it with you guys. If you enjoy any of my recipies, feel free to leave a comment.

I grew up in California and I currently am an undergrad at Princeton University. Ever since I was a kid, I have always loved cooking. I saw it as both a science and an art. I love watching people's faces light up whenever they try what I make.

I created this blog to be relatable. You can ask anyone who knows me, I am a humongous clutz. My recipies are not about making something pretty, rather, they're to make something absolutely and positively delicious! If I can pull through, so can you!
